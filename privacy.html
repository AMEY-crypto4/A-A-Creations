Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026. This explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. What we collect

When you use our contact/audit request form, WhatsApp, or email us directly, we collect the information you choose to share — typically your name, business name, phone number, email address, and details about your current website. We do not collect this information through any hidden tracking or third-party data broker.

2. Why we collect it

We use this information solely to: respond to your audit or quote request, communicate about an active or potential project, send you your quote/invoice, and provide ongoing support if you become a client. We do not use your information for unrelated marketing without your separate consent.

3. How we store it

Information is stored in our project tracking tools and email/WhatsApp systems, accessible only to A&A Creations. We take reasonable steps to keep this information secure but cannot guarantee absolute security of any data transmitted over the internet.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except: (a) with service providers strictly necessary to deliver your project (e.g. a hosting or payment provider), or (b) where required by law.

5. Cookies & website analytics

Our own website may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand traffic (e.g. page views). We do not use invasive tracking or sell browsing data to advertisers.

6. Your rights

You can ask us at any time to: tell you what information we hold about you, correct inaccurate information, or delete your information from our records (except where we're required to retain records for legal/accounting purposes, such as invoices). Contact hello@aacreations.com for any of these requests.

7. Data retention

We retain client project information for as long as needed to provide support and to meet accounting/legal obligations, and delete lead/inquiry information that did not convert to a project after a reasonable period.

8. Children's privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and are not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time; the current version will always be available at this page.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to hello@aacreations.com.

This page is a general template intended to reflect good data-handling practice under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and does not constitute legal advice. We recommend having this reviewed by a qualified professional as your data handling needs grow.
