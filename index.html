The signs

How to tell your site is holding you back

If any of these sound familiar, you're likely losing customers to a competitor whose site simply looks more trustworthy.

01

It's a single page

Everything crammed onto one scroll — no dedicated pages for services, pricing, or reviews for search engines to find.

02

Built on a free builder

Wix, Weebly, or a "sites.google.com" address. Fine to start — but it caps how professional and fast your site can be.

03

Broken on mobile

Over 60% of local searches happen on a phone. If your site isn't built mobile-first, most visitors leave in seconds.

04

No clear way to contact you

No booking form, no click-to-call, no map. Visitors shouldn't have to hunt for how to reach you.

05

Slow to load

Old templates and unoptimized images can take 8+ seconds to load. Most visitors won't wait past 3.

06

Invisible to AI search

Customers increasingly ask ChatGPT or Google's AI Overviews for recommendations. Old sites without structured content don't get surfaced there at all.
