1. Who these terms cover

2. Scope & quotes

Every project begins with a written quote describing the exact scope, package or add-ons included, timeline, and price. Work only begins once you approve the quote in writing (email or signed quote document) and pay the agreed deposit. Any request outside the approved scope will be quoted separately before we proceed — we do not bill for unapproved extra work.

3. Payment terms

Unless otherwise agreed in writing: 50% of the project price is due upfront as a deposit to begin work, and the remaining 50% is due before final files and domain handover. Add-ons and monthly Care/Growth Plans are billed as described in your quote. Prices are quoted in INR; approximate foreign-currency figures shown anywhere are for reference only and are not binding.

4. Revisions

Each package includes a set number of revision rounds (see your quote or the Pricing page). A "revision round" means a consolidated set of change requests reviewed together. Additional rounds beyond what's included are billed at the add-on rate listed on our Pricing page at the time of request.

5. Cancellation & refund policy

  • Before work begins: if you cancel before any design or development work has started, your deposit is refunded in full, less any payment gateway fees already incurred.
  • After work has started: the deposit becomes non-refundable once work begins, as it covers time and resources already committed. If you cancel mid-project, you will be invoiced for work completed beyond the deposit amount, calculated on a pro-rata basis against the total quoted price.
  • After delivery: once the final site is delivered and the balance payment is made, the engagement is considered complete and is not eligible for a refund. Post-launch issues are covered instead by your package's free support window.

6. Timelines

Delivery estimates shown on our Pricing page (e.g. 5–7 days, 10–14 days) assume timely feedback and content from you (photos, text, logo, etc.). Delays in providing requested materials will extend the delivery date accordingly.

7. Ownership & intellectual property

Full ownership of the final source code, design files, and content created specifically for your project transfers to you upon receipt of full and final payment. Until final payment is received, all work remains the property of A&A Creations. Any third-party assets used (stock photography, fonts, plugins) remain subject to their own original licenses, which we will disclose if applicable.

8. What's not included by default

Unless explicitly listed in your quote, the following are not included: domain registration cost, ongoing hosting cost, stock photography licensing, copywriting, logo design, paid advertising, and ongoing SEO/marketing services. These are all available as add-ons.

9. Post-launch support

Each package includes a free support window (7, 30, or 60 days depending on tier) covering bug fixes and minor tweaks to what was delivered. It does not cover new features, new pages, or content changes beyond minor text/image swaps. After the support window, ongoing help is available via a paid Care Plan or per-request billing.
